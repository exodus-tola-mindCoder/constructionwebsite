Our Services

At Gelana Dechasa General construction we offer a comprehensive range of construction services designed to meet your every need. From the initial planning stages to the final touches, our team is dedicated to delivering excellence at every step. We combine industry expertise with a commitment to quality to ensure that each project is completed on time, within budget, and to the highest standards.Our services are tailored to provide you with a seamless experience, whether you’re starting a new build, renovating an existing space, or maintaining your property. Explore how our specialized services can help bring your vision to life and build a strong foundation for your future.

Site Preparation

Getting your site ready for construction is crucial to the success of any project. Our site preparation services ensure that your land is properly cleared, leveled, and ready for the next phase. From clearing debris to grading the land, we handle everything to create a solid foundation for your build. We take care of the groundwork so you can focus on what comes next.

Interior design

Your space should reflect your style and needs. Our interior designing services help bring your vision to life, creating spaces that are both beautiful and functional. Whether you’re looking to redesign a single room or an entire building, we work closely with you to craft interiors that are tailored to your taste and lifestyle. From concept to completion, we focus on every detail to ensure your space is a true reflection of you.

Preconstruction services

Planning is the key to a successful project. Our preconstruction services help you map out every detail before the work begins. We assist with budgeting, scheduling, and identifying potential challenges to ensure your project runs smoothly. By taking care of the groundwork upfront, we help you avoid costly surprises later on and keep your project on track from day one.

Building Constructions

Turning your vision into reality is our specialty. Our building construction services cover everything from laying the foundation to the final touches. Whether it’s a residential, commercial, or industrial project, we bring skilled craftsmanship and attention to detail to every phase of construction. We’re committed to delivering high-quality results on time and within budget, ensuring that your project stands tall for years to come.

maintenance services

Keeping your property in top condition is essential for its longevity and safety. Our maintenance services provide regular upkeep and repairs to ensure everything functions as it should. From routine inspections to fixing issues before they become major problems, we help you protect your investment and keep your property running smoothly.

Constructions management

Effective management is key to a successful construction project. Our construction management services ensure that every aspect of your project is coordinated and executed smoothly. From planning and budgeting to overseeing the day-to-day operations, we handle it all. We keep projects on track, on time, and within budget, so you can focus on your goals while we handle the details.
